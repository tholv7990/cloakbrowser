01 — Why the current values read legacy
Muddy tone text

Current fg values (#046B4C · #B61F1F · #974608) are dark and desaturated — 2015 enterprise. Modern tones are one step brighter and cleaner in hue.

Gray-tinged tints

The 07-20 tints (#ECF5F0 · #FBF3E6) went near-neutral — safe but lifeless. The modern register is a clean, cold wash with visible hue.

Hand-mixed dark solids

Dark tints as opaque swatches (#1E2F27) only work on one surface. Alpha tints (~14%) sit correctly on card, body, and popover alike — your TMS trial already proved this.

Three palettes, one product

WMS/OMS run old VELAS_PALETTE, TMS runs the SaaS trial. Same StatusChip, different greens. This sheet unifies them; accents stay per-app (§3.7 cobalt/violet/cyan).
